MAPO TOFU

Tender tofu cooked in an umami and spicy sauce, accompanied by minced meat, Mapo tofu is one of the tastiest ways to prepare this healthy ingredient.

Mapo tofu (麻婆豆腐, ma po dou fu) is one of the most popular dishes from Sichuan cuisine. Sichuan is known for bold flavours and unapolegtic spices, mapo tofu being a key representative dish that is now known worldwide.

When I think of comfort foods, I think of the recipes that my mom and grandparents cooked while growing up; Mapo Tofu is one of the first recipes that come into mind. It is a savory and spicy dish that will have you downing bowls and bowls of rice.
